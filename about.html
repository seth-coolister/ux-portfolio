About Me

Hi, I’m Seth Callister — a UX/UI designer who loves turning tricky problems into clean, intuitive experiences. My background in psychology helps me see how people actually think and behave, which makes my designs not just pretty, but genuinely useful.

I’ve worked on everything from eCommerce sites to fintech platforms, leading projects end-to-end or jumping in to collaborate with developers and stakeholders. Tools like Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and Webflow are where I do my best work, but my real focus is creating products that feel effortless for the people using them.

At the heart of my process are a few simple values: communicate clearly, stay curious, and never lose sight of the human on the other side of the screen.
